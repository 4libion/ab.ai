After COVID-19, not everyone could afford programming courses, yet many talented programmers in our country could contribute. With more children gaining access to laptops, I aim to develop IT education in villages as a meaningful and productive after-school activity.

Besides technology, I play the dombra and believe that music helps in learning programming - the rhythm and structure of kuys resemble coding logic and foster creativity.

About

Ab.ai is the largest platform for mentored programming training in Kazakhstan. This project unites students and teachers interested in IT from all over the country.

Ab.ai was created with the aim of popularizing the profession of the future by creating a community of like-minded people. We sincerely believe that Ab.ai will contribute to strengthening the competitiveness of Kazakhstan in the global information technology market.

The peculiarity of our approach is the use of dombra music as a tool for developing logical thinking, attention and creativity – skills that directly help in learning programming. The rhythm and structure of the kuis are similar to the algorithms in the code, which makes the learning process more understandable and exciting.

Most importantly, anyone can become part of our community absolutely free of charge, choosing the path of a student or mentor.

What does the student receive upon completion of the course?

Strong theoretical basis

Own projects - from simple to complex

Practical knowledge and skills in the chosen programming area

A unique learning experience where dombra music accelerates programming

How does our platform work?

You register and choose your position (student/mentor)

All your information is processed in the database, and we select a student or teacher for you.

Throughout the course you will have a supervisor who will help you if necessary.

  • Some training programs use the “Programming through dombra” method, where musical rhythms and the structure of kuys help to understand the algorithms and logic of the code more easily.

Why is it worth learning programming through dombra?

Combines creativity and logic

Playing the dombra develops a sense of rhythm and creativity, which helps to understand algorithms and build code more easily.

Strengthens memory and attention

Learning kuis trains concentration and the ability to work with large amounts of information.

Makes learning fun

Musical associations help you learn complex topics faster and maintain interest in programming.

Develops discipline and teamwork

In both an ensemble and a team of programmers, consistency and understanding of the general rhythm are important.
